Welcome!

I hope that you enjoy my creativity and energy. My love of beauty in nature and color is what truly inspires me. Most of my artwork is created by the thrill of exploring and experimenting. What excites me is..... to see what will happen next, oooh the possibilities! When I paint, I just let go and trust in myself. I have devoted my artistic talents to creating paintings that are full of memories!
